# Haven AI Partners and Recursive Form Strategic Partnership to Advance Practical AI Adoption in Japan

## Combining Haven’s distribution-first platform for Japan go-to-market with Recursive’s world-class AI engineering and OpenAI-powered models to help companies move from pilots to measurable business outcomes

Haven AI Partners Inc. (“Haven”), a Japan-based AI distribution and execution partner, has entered into a strategic partnership with Recursive Inc. (“Recursive”), a leading AI development company. Together, Haven and Recursive will help Japanese enterprises and SMBs deploy production-grade AI safely and reliably in day-to-day operations \- moving from pilots to sustained, measurable business impact.

The partnership combines Haven’s growing distribution footprint \- built through a buy-and-build strategy that brings trusted domestic IT service providers (MSPs, VARs, and SIers) into an AI-native group \- with Recursive’s OpenAI-powered global-standard AI engineering. For best-in-class AI solution companies, Haven aims to be the first call for Japan go-to-market: a trusted local operator that can drive distribution, localization, and long-term execution.

The partnership provides a faster, lower-risk path to Japan revenue for global AI companies, while it also strengthens Haven’s execution engine and distribution moat in a buy-and-build strategy designed for asymmetric returns.

### **Turning AI from Experiments into Execution**

Japan has strong demand for AI, but many organizations still struggle to push beyond pilots. The limiting factor is rarely the model \- it is execution: choosing the right use cases, integrating AI into real workflows, meeting security and governance requirements, and operating systems reliably at scale.

Haven and Recursive are addressing this gap by delivering AI solutions that are built for production from day one. Recursive brings deep research capability and proven delivery experience, and has announced a technical collaboration with OpenAI last year to develop customer solutions utilizing the latest AI models. Recursive’s work includes deployments with leading Japanese organizations such as IHI, KDDI, and Suntory Group companies — demonstrating its ability to deliver production-grade AI in complex, regulated environments. Haven brings hands-on operating expertise and a distribution network rooted in long-standing relationships with Japanese businesses and IT service providers \- enabling faster adoption and higher success rates in the field.

Haven and Recursive believe the next phase of AI adoption is about execution. That means understanding how businesses in Japan actually operate, working closely with decision-makers, and building AI systems that can run in production environments.

### **A Distribution Layer Built for Japan Go-to-Market**

In Japan, trusted channels matter. Many enterprise and mid-market customers rely on MSPs and SI partners to select, implement, and run technology in production. Haven is consolidating this channel layer into a single, AI-native platform \- creating a repeatable distribution path for modern AI solutions.

Haven operates as a long-term holding company (not a time-bound fund). It acquires high-quality IT service providers that serve as the trusted channel layer for Japanese enterprises, preserves what makes them trusted locally, and embeds AI agents and automation to expand capabilities and margins. The model is designed for an asymmetric profile \- downside supported by cash-flowing operations, with uncapped upside through platform leverage and cross-sell.

As part of this partnership, Haven will open a structured Japan go-to-market program for AI companies, including co-selling through Haven’s network, localization and compliance support, and joint solution engineering and integration with Recursive. Haven will provide AI companies a faster path to Japan revenue through Haven’s distribution layer; production-grade delivery with governance and long-term operations.

### **How the Partnership Works**

Under the partnership, Haven and Recursive will collaborate with clearly defined roles to ensure speed, quality, and accountability across the full lifecycle \- from identifying high-value opportunities to operating solutions in production:

Haven AI Partners will focus on:

* Originating and qualifying opportunities through its executive network and IT service provider footprint across Japan (SMB to enterprise)  
* Clarifying business problems and where AI can realistically help  
* Leading go-to-market execution: use-case definition, stakeholder alignment, localization, and long-term customer success

Recursive will focus on:

* Designing and building AI solutions using advanced AI models  
* Deploying AI systems that are stable, secure, and ready for long-term use  
* Bringing global AI knowledge and best practices into the Japanese market

### **Looking Ahead**

Haven and Recursive will begin by deploying AI solutions across Haven’s group companies and customer base, building a repeatable playbook for implementation. Over time, the partners plan to expand solution packages across common enterprise workflows and to support additional global AI companies seeking a reliable Japan distribution and execution partner. As global AI adoption accelerates, Japan’s largest enterprises — from industrial conglomerates to global consumer brands — are seeking partners who can deliver AI reliably at scale.

Haven will continue forming partnerships with leading AI companies in Japan and abroad, with the goal of becoming the first-in-mind distribution and execution layer for the Japan AI market. Investors and partners who share this long-term view are invited to connect.
